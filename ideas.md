**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets modern SaaS consulting

**Core Principles:**
1. Structured asymmetry — content blocks offset on a strict invisible grid
2. Typography as the primary visual element — large, confident headlines
3. Monochromatic with a single warm accent (deep teal/slate)
4. Generous whitespace as a signal of premium positioning

**Color Philosophy:**
Deep navy (#0F1B2D) as the primary background for hero sections, off-white (#F7F6F2) for body, and a warm amber (#E8A045) as the sole accent. The contrast signals trust and precision — qualities a BA brings to every engagement.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Horizontal rule dividers separate sections. Headlines bleed left while body copy sits in a right-aligned column. Navigation is a slim top bar with the logo left and CTA right — no hamburger menus on desktop.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rules with section numbers (01, 02, 03) in small caps
- Large display numbers for stats (e.g., "12+ years", "40+ products delivered")
- Monospace font for technical terms (e.g., `user stories`, `backlog`)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Understated — hover states use underline reveals, not color floods. Scroll-triggered fade-ins are slow and deliberate.

**Animation:**
Fade-up on scroll (opacity 0→1, translateY 20px→0, 600ms ease-out). No parallax. CTA button has a subtle border-draw animation on hover.

**Typography System:**
- Display: DM Serif Display (italic for emphasis)
- Body: DM Sans (400, 500)
- Mono: JetBrains Mono (for technical labels)

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Editorial Minimalism — inspired by high-end consulting firm annual reports

**Core Principles:**
1. Extreme whitespace — sections breathe with 120px+ vertical padding
2. Left-anchored layout — content lives in the left 60% on desktop
3. Subtle warm neutrals — no pure white, no pure black
4. Photography and illustration are secondary to typography

**Color Philosophy:**
Warm white (#FAFAF8) background, charcoal (#1C1C1E) text, slate blue (#3B5BDB) as the primary action color, and soft sage (#E8EDE7) for section backgrounds. The palette feels calm, credible, and human — not corporate-cold.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Split hero: large headline left, a clean abstract visual right. Services presented as a horizontal scrolling card row on desktop. "How It Works" uses a vertical timeline with alternating left/right content.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized section labels in light gray (e.g., "SERVICES" as a watermark behind the headline)
- Thin vertical accent lines beside blockquotes and testimonials
- Pill-shaped tags for service categories

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Smooth and intentional — all transitions 300ms ease. Cards lift with a soft shadow on hover. CTA button fills with color on hover using a left-to-right wipe.

**Animation:**
Staggered fade-up for card grids. Hero headline splits by word and animates in sequentially. Scroll progress indicator in the top bar.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (bold, for hero headlines)
- Body: Lato (400, 600)
- Labels: Lato (700, uppercase, tracked)

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Structured Brutalism meets Consulting Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. Bold typographic hierarchy — headlines at 80px+, no apologies
2. Hard geometric shapes used as decorative elements (rectangles, not blobs)
3. High contrast sections — alternating dark/light panels
4. Content density is a feature — no filler, every word earns its place

**Color Philosophy:**
Near-black (#111318) for dark sections, crisp white (#FFFFFF) for light sections, and electric indigo (#4F46E5) as the primary accent. A secondary warm gold (#D4A843) for highlights. The palette feels decisive and energetic — matching the pace of startup consulting.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Full-width alternating panels. Hero is full-bleed dark with a large headline and a single CTA. Services section uses a 2-column grid with bold numbered headings. "How It Works" is a horizontal step-by-step flow with connecting lines.

**Signature Elements:**
- Geometric rectangle accents (thin colored bars beside headings)
- Bold step numbers in a contrasting color block
- Footer with a large "Let's Work Together" CTA spanning full width

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Decisive — hover states are immediate (no slow transitions). Buttons have a hard drop-shadow that shifts on hover (press effect).

**Animation:**
Slide-in from left for headlines, fade-in for body. Counter animations for stats. No scroll-jacking.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk (700, 800)
- Body: Inter (400, 500)
- Accent: Space Mono (for code-like labels)

## Selected Approach

**Chosen: Editorial Minimalism (Response 2)**

Rationale: The warm, editorial aesthetic best suits a fractional consulting brand. It signals premium positioning without being cold or overly corporate. The left-anchored layout and generous whitespace communicate confidence and clarity — exactly what a Business Analyst brings to client engagements.
